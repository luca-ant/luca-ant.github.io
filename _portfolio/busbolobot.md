A *Telegram bot* for buses timetables in Bologna. \\
It is written in **Python3** and relies on the [HelloBus Tper](https://www.tper.it/hello-bus) service.

You can find it [here](https://telegram.me/busbolobot) or searching **@busbolobot**  on Telegram! \\
Useful to get continuous notifications when you are late!
