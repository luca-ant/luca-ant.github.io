*TeamPallo* is an AI [tablut](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tafl_games#Tablut) player written in **Java** and it is based on MinMax algorithm.\\
It has been developed for the [challenge]((http://ai.unibo.it/games/boardgamecompetition/tablut/1819INF)) of the Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence course hosted by prof. Paola Mello at University of Bologna.
