The *Sorting Hat* is a sentient hat at Hogwarts that magically determines which of the four school Houses each new student belongs most to. In this quiz game you have to answer some questions using eyes, looking left or right, and the Sorting Hat will assign you the House.

*Sorting Hat* is written in **Python3**. \\
Computer Vision algorithms provided by **OpenCV** library have been used for eye and pupil detection.
