Deep Comedy is an **Natural Language Generation** application aimed at reproducing Dante Alighieri's hendecasyllable structure and rhyming scheme.. \\
Working with **Tensorflow** and using **RNN**, we trained several kind of networks to simulate how humans compose poetry.
